repairs

At Assens Shipyard all our workshops are in close proximity. It’s a small and charming yard, but we managed to fit all the equipment needed to build a ship. We have 3 cranes. A 18 ton crane with a 75m overhang and two mobile cranes at 20- and 30 ton to lift the heavy materials, and smaller cranes and trucks to assemble the ship. We have two floating docks. The only covered dock in Denmark at 80m and a width of 14m with a lifting capacity of 1200 ton. The open dock at 100m and width of 18,5m.
